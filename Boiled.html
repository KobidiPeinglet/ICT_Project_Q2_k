Ingredients:

6/12 large eggs

salt

Water

Instructions:


  1. For soft boiled eggs cook for 5 mins, For medium boiled eggs cook for 8 mins, For hard boiled eggs cook for 12 mins

  2. Place eggs in a saucepan or pot and cover with cold water.

  3. Put pan over high heat and bring water to a rolling boil. Remove pan from heat and cover.

  4. Drain eggs immediately and put in a bowl filled with water and ice cubes.
