Ingredients:

3 large eggs

1 teaspoon olive oil

Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper

Toast, for serving, optional

Instructions:


  1. Heat the oil in a medium nonstick skillet over low heat until slightly shimmering.

  2. About 5 minutes. Crack an egg into a small ramekin and slowly add it to the skillet; repeat with the other egg, adding it to the other side of the skillet.

  3. Cover with a tight lid and cook, uninterrupted, until the whites are completely set but the yolks are still runny, 2 to 2 1/2 minutes.

  4. Slide the eggs out of the skillet onto a plate or toast. Season with salt and pepper.
