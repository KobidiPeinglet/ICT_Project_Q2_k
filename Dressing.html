Ingredients:

2 eggs, beaten

1/2 cup sugar

1/4 cup water

1/4 cup white vinegar

8 cups mixed salad greens

1 small onion, chopped

4 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled

1 medium tomato, cut into wedges

1/2 cup sliced cucumber

Instructions:


  1. In a microwave-safe bowl, combine the eggs, sugar and water. Microwave, uncovered, on high for 45 seconds. Stir in vinegar; cook for 1 to 1-1/2 minutes or until a thermometer reads 160Â°.

  2. Meanwhile, in a salad bowl, combine greens, onion, bacon, tomato and cucumber. Drizzle with warm dressing; toss to coat.
